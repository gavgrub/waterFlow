About

SSR Water Flow by Gavin Grubert is a website which allows users to view information about the South Saskatchewan River, and more broadly, the South Saskatchewan River Basin.

This project was made for the South East Alberta Watershed Alliance (SEAWA) to help visualize the watershed and its tributaries.
